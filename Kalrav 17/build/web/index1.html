About Sympulse

We present to you, Pune's biggest college fest. Sympulse, with its inception in the year 2009, is the international student fest of Symbiosis Centre for Management Studies, Pune. Spread across a span of five days, the fest provides students from junior and under graduate colleges a chance to participate and explore. Be it an exposure into the different worlds of business and management, a chance to discuss world issues at an international model UN conference, participating and showcasing talent in a cultural extravaganza or showing their competitive spirit through sports, Sympulse has it all. With immense and growing national and international participation, Sympulse aims at being one of the premier fests of the country for an unforgettable experience for all.
